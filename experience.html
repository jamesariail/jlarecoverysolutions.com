Key Projects

Selected Project Experience

City of Asheville CDBG-DR Action Plan

City of Asheville, NC | 2024–2025

Led development of City's first CDBG-DR Action Plan for its $225M allocation following Hurricane Helene, the most destructive storms to hit the region in modern history.

  • Developed Action Plan and received HUD approval within 90 days of Allocation Announcement Notice with no required changes
  • Worked with multiple City stakeholders, including the City Manager, Mayor and City Council members, to design programs and develop a method of distribution that dovetailed with other funding sources to meet complex recovery requirements
  • Designed programs for housing, infrastructure, and economic recovery
  • Supported the City in augmenting its procurement and financial management policies and procedures to successfully receive financial and grants management certification from HUD

Nebraska CDBG-DR Program Management

Nebraska Department of Economic Development | 2020–2025

Led Action Plan development and comprehensive grants management and implementation support for $108M in CDBG-DR funding following severe flooding events.

  • Developed Action Plan and subsequent amendments
  • Led implementation over lifecycle of grant
  • Created Infrastructure Match Program integrating PA and HMGP
  • Implemented housing programs including Low-Income Housing Tax Credit (LIHTC) "piggyback" program for affordable multifamily housing development

Louisiana ERAP Grants Administration

Louisiana Housing Corporation | 2021–2022

Led case management, eligibility review, and policy development for statewide Emergency Rental Assistance Program (ERAP) during the COVID-19 pandemic.

  • Worked directly with Louisiana Office of Community Development leadership providing advisory support on policy issues and adjudicating edge cases
  • Developed comprehensive policy framework including DOB verification procedures
  • Led efforts to design and implement staffing model, training and business processes for intake, eligibility review, quality control, appeals, and compliance monitoring

Texas GLO Alternative Housing Study

Texas General Land Office | 2019–2025

Provided executive and technical leadership for multi-year study evaluating innovative housing technologies for disaster recovery applications.

  • Led evaluation of 3D printing, modular, and other emerging construction technologies
  • Supported laboratory testing of top-performing solutions with Texas A&M
  • Resulted in multiple task order awards over five-year period
